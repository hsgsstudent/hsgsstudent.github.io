Welcome to my homepage! My name is Nguyen Hoang Viet, and I am currently a final-year Computer Science student at [Hanoi University of Science and Technology](https://hust.edu.vn). My research focuses on the interpretability of Mixture-of-Experts models, particularly the theory of gating mechanisms and expert architectures, under the supervision of Professor [Nhat Ho](https://nhatptnk8912.github.io).
